The internet refers to the global network of computers that are interconnected and communicate with each other 
using a common set of protocols. It is a vast network that allows people to access and share information, 
communicate with others, and perform various tasks such as online shopping, banking, research, entertainment, 
and much more. The internet enables the transfer of data, files, and multimedia across different devices and 
geographical locations, making it an essential part of modern life.
